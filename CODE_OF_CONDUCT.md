# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Being respectful and inclusive
- Giving and receiving constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, insults, discriminatory language, and personal attacks.

## Enforcement
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior.

## Reporting
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, open a private security report or contact maintainers.

This document is adapted from the Contributor Covenant (v2.1).
